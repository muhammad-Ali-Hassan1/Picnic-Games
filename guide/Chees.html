How to Play Chees:

  1. Chess Board SetupThe board is an 8x8 grid of alternating light and dark squares. Place the board so that each player has a white square on their right-hand corner.
  2. Basic Rules of MovementPawns move forward one square but capture diagonally. On their first move, pawns can move forward two squares.
  3. The Goal of the GameThe objective is to checkmate your opponent's king, which means the king is under attack and has no legal moves to escape the attack.
  4. Special MovesA move involving the king and a rook. The king moves two squares towards the rook, and the rook jumps to the square next to the king on the opposite side. This move is only allowed if neither piece has moved previously, the squares between them are empty, and the king is not in check.
  5. Taking TurnsPlayers alternate turns, moving one piece at a time. White always moves first.

Equipment Required for Chees

  4. Notation Paper and Pen (Optional)
