Foosball

Foosball is a fun and fast-paced game that simulates soccer, but with players controlled by rods and figures mounted on a table. Here's a basic guide on how to play foosball

How to Play Foosball:

  1. Set Up the Foosball TableA typical foosball table has two teams of 1 to 4 players each. Most commonly, there are two or four people playing, with each controlling one or two rods.
  2. Basic SetupPlace the ball in the center of the table. Divide the players into two teams, with each team either controlling one or two rods.
  3. Starting the GameA coin toss can determine which team serves first, or you can agree on another way. The game begins by serving the ball from the center circle. To serve, launch the ball by placing it in the center and rolling it down the table to get it in play.
  4. Basic Rules for PlayingYou control the players (figures) on the rods by turning, pushing, or pulling them. The rods can rotate 360°, but you can only move them back and forth along their axis.
  5. Scoring a Goal As mentioned, spinning the rods is not allowed.
