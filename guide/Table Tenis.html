How to Play Table Tenis:

  1. Starting the Game:The server must toss the ball into the air and hit it so that it first bounces on their side of the table and then crosses the net, landing on the opponent's side. After the first serve, the opponent must return the ball by hitting it with their racket before it bounces twice on their side.
  2. Rally:Once the ball is served, the opponent must return the ball by hitting it back over the net before it bounces twice on their side. During the rally, players must take turns hitting the ball over the net. The ball must always land on the table on the opponentâ€™s side.

Scoring a Point:A point is scored when the opponent fails to return the ball, hit the ball correctly, keep the ball from going out of bounds and let the ball bounce twice on their side of the table. For example, if your opponent misses the ball, hits it out of bounds, or lets it bounce twice, you earn a point.
  4. Winning the Game:The game is won when a player reaches 11 points, provided they lead by at least 2 points. If both players are tied at 10 points, the game continues until one player has a 2-point advantage (e.g., 12-10 or 13-11).
  5. Changing Sides:After each game, players change sides. If the match is being played to a third game (in a best-of-three format), players switch sides when one player reaches 5 points in the final game.
