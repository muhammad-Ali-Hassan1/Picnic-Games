GAMES FOR KIDS


Blind man's buff

Blind man's buff or blind man's bluff is a variant of tag in which the player who is "It" (the person who is tagging others) is blindfolded. The traditional name of the game is "blind man's buff", where the word buff is used in its older sense of a small push.

Horseshoes

Horseshoes is a lawn game played between two people (or two teams of two people) using four horseshoes and two throwing targets (stakes) set in a lawn or sandbox area.

Musical chairs

A set of chairs is arranged in a circle with one fewer chair than the number of players. While music plays, the contestants walk around the set of chairs. When the music stops abruptly, all players must find their own individual chair to sit on.

Egg-and-spoon race

All you need is four friends up for the challenge and you can see who 'cracks' under the pressure! The first player to reach the finish line without dropping their egg is the winner. Remember you can only use one hand to balance the egg on the spoon.

Sandcastle Art

Sand art is the practice of modelling sand into an artistic form, such as sand brushing, sand sculpting, sand painting, or creating sand bottles. A sandcastle is a type of sand sculpture resembling a miniature building, often a castle.

Beach Frisbee Golf

Beach Frisbee Golf combines the fun of frisbee with the challenge of golf, using natural beach elements as targets. Players aim to complete the course with as few throws as possible, making it a perfect game for all ages.

Relay Race

A kids' relay race is a fun, team-based competition where participants take turns running between designated points, passing a baton or object to the next runner. It's a fast-paced, energetic game that encourages teamwork and friendly competition!
